Come Visit Long Island

Known for its scenic coastlines and cultural diversity, Long Island lies just east of New York City.

Essential Long Island Experiences

Patchogue

Experience the vibrant nightlife, dining, and shopping in the heart of Patchogue.

Montauk

Visit the historic Montauk Lighthouse and enjoy stunning ocean views.

Fire Island

Relax on secluded beaches and explore the natural beauty of Fire Island.
